Chinatown

The restaurants of Chinatown are extremely delicious, reasonably priced and offer a wide variety of regional Chinese cuisine. Finding a delicious meal in Chinatown will be even easier the next time you visit.

Nob Hill

Perhaps one of the most famous neighborhoods in San Francisco, mainly for the steep slopes that tourists and locals regularly trek, Nob Hill is a haven for all kinds of cuisine.

South Market

In SoMa, it’s like walking through a loud funhouse where, by pulling a hidden lever, a garage becomes a sports bar.

Haight

Ton of far less cliché reasons for local San Franciscans to make their way to the notorious street, like a killer jazz club, tasty street tacos, and some of the best craft cocktails around.

Mission

Even food and drink in the historically Latin American neighborhood can be divisive, but this list aims to offer something for every kind of diner.

Tenderloin

From upscale French food and jazz clubs to affordable Senegalese and Vietnamese food, the Tenderloin is indeed the mark of any proper city

Excelsior

On the south side of the city, Excelsior is a vast neighborhood filled with underrated restaurants.

Embarcadero

Day in and day out, the pleasant stretch attracts crowds of both tourists and locals, who come for the weekly farmers’ markets, the wide sidewalks for strolling, the fresh air — and yes, the food and drinks.

Castro

The Castro is home to plenty of good food: reliable spots for weekend brunch and date night wine and tapas, family-run dumpling and noodle spots, and an old standby for SF classic ice cream.

Sunset

The former expanse of dunes became a community of Irish and Asian American families, a final destination for surfers around the world, and, slowly, a veritable buried treasure of places to eat and drink.

Persidio

Located at the Golden Gate, the Presidio is a national park site like no other, with surprising adventures for everyone

Portola

A city within the big city, Portola Place is a microcosm of San Francisco itself, complete with neighborhood favorite dining spots that are worth a planned visit
